# Privacy Policy – BergRebels AI Chat Assistant

_Last updated: November 2025_

## 1. Introduction

The **BergRebels AI Chat Assistant** is a browser extension that helps users discover, plan, and book tourism and leisure activities.  
We value your privacy and are committed to handling all user data securely and transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

This extension **does not collect any personal information** such as your name, email address, or account identifiers.

The only data transmitted are **chat messages** that you voluntarily enter when interacting with the AI assistant.  
These messages are sent to our backend API:

https://ttf-backend-665542325765.europe-central2.run.app/agent

for the sole purpose of generating relevant responses and activity suggestions.

We do **not**:
- Collect or store personal identifiers
- Access your browsing history
- Use cookies or analytics trackers
- Share data with third parties

---

## 3. How Your Data Is Used

Your chat messages are processed **in real time** and **not stored** beyond the duration of the request.  
The messages are used exclusively to generate a helpful AI response related to travel planning or activity recommendations.

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.  
All communications between your browser and our backend are protected by HTTPS encryption.

---

## 5. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.  
Since only anonymized chat messages are transmitted, the privacy risk is minimal.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Because no personal or identifiable data is collected or stored, no account, deletion, or correction actions are required.  
If you have any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us via email.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or changes in our services.  
All updates will be published on the same page, and the “Last updated” date will indicate the latest revision.
